# Knowledge Gates — N01: The Grip That Holds

## Dual Canon System

The OmniMeta Saga operates under a **dual canon architecture**:
- **Innocent Frame (N01-N29):** Characters unaware of divine identities and orchestration
- **Guilty Frame (N30):** All secrets revealed, orchestrator identity confirmed

N01 operates STRICTLY under **innocent frame rules**.

---

## Innocent Frame: What Characters Know (N01)

### Observable Facts
- **The Piercing:** A massive object transited Te'Oga, entering one side and exiting near Saint Merro
- **Spindle:** Dark absence with bent-light boundary visible during transit
- **The Seam:** Persistent sky-scar visible post-Piercing
- **Gravity Disruption:** Brief reduction followed by harsh return
- **Pressure Wall:** Chest compression before audible roar
- **Copper-Gold Particulate:** Sticky metallic dust coating everything post-Piercing
- **Wrong Grid-Hum:** Altered baseline vibration in infrastructure (persistent)
- **Soulpulse Manifestation:** Some people can generate copper-gold glow through contact
- **Visible Costs:** Tremor, vein-lines, exhaustion, sensory overload after Soulpulse use

### Working Theories (Characters May Speculate)
- Infrastructure stress patterns differ post-Piercing
- Soulpulse users share similar cost markers
- Conventional bracing + timing protocols reduce casualties
- Political institutions resist safety measures that slow throughput
- Some failures are predictable with proper measurement

### Explicit Uncertainties (Characters Acknowledge They Don't Know)
- Why the Piercing happened
- What the spindle/Seam truly represent
- Why some people manifest Soulpulse and others don't
- Whether costs will escalate indefinitely
- If the Piercing will repeat or worsen

---

## Guilty Frame: What Characters DON'T Know (Hidden Until N30)

### Divine Identities
- **Jhace Torrins = Forge** (Divine Principle of metalwork, structure, endurance)
- **Tiffani Merrow = Glass** (Divine Principle of precision, clarity, fragility)
- **Kylar = Cord** (Divine Principle of connection, tension, binding)
- **Bram = Anvil** (Divine Principle of resistance, grounding, refusal)
- Other god-vessels exist but are unaware of their status

### Orchestration
- **Counter-Spark / The Auditor:** Observing entity who orchestrated the Piercing
- **Purpose:** Force Stasis to end, trigger god-vessel awakening
- **Coffee Ring Records:** Auditor's observation logs (clinically tracking god progression)
- **"Observation continues":** Auditor's framing of ongoing god development

### Metaphysics
- **Divine Principles:** Eight conscious entities governing fundamental forces
- **God-Vessels:** Mortals carrying dormant Principles, unaware until late-stage awakening
- **Soulpulse Origin:** Manifestation of Divine Principle energy through mortal vessel
- **Cost Mechanism:** Mortal body resisting divine energy expression
- **Stasis:** Ancient enforced peace that the Piercing was designed to break
- **Fathom Break:** Later event where god-vessels begin to suspect their nature

## N01 Knowledge Gate Enforcement

### ALLOWED in N01 Prose
- Characters discuss observable Piercing consequences
- Characters theorize about infrastructure behavior changes
- Characters note Soulpulse manifestation and costs
- Characters see the Seam but do not explain it
- Characters experience wrong grid-hum without cosmic framing
- Characters develop protocols based on empirical observation

### FORBIDDEN in N01 Prose
- Any character awareness of god-vessel status
- Any mention of Divine Principles as entities
- Any reference to Counter-Spark, Auditor, or orchestration
- Any implication that Piercing was intentional/purposeful
- Any suggestion that Soulpulse is "divine" or "mystical"
- Any cosmic significance attributed to the Seam
- Any "this was meant to happen" framing
- Any character suspicion of being watched/guided

### THE SEAM Handling (Critical)
The Seam is visible in N01 as:
- A bent-light scar across the sky
- Persistent environmental marker
- Observable fact like "the sun rose"

Characters may:
- Look at it
- Note its presence
- Reference it as landmark ("visible in clear gap")

Characters may NOT:
- Theorize about its metaphysical meaning
- Suggest it connects to anything beyond visible scar
- Treat it as mystical or portentous
- Discuss what it "means" beyond "the sky broke"

**Example ALLOWED:**
> The Seam was visible in a clear air gap above the roofline. Both looked up once. Neither spoke. They returned to the brace work.

**Example FORBIDDEN:**
> The Seam hung above them like a cosmic wound, as if the gods themselves had torn reality. Jhace wondered if it watched them, if something beyond the sky cared about their struggle.

## Knowledge Progression Across Saga

**N01-N05 (Turning 1 - GLASS):**
- Innocent frame maintained
- Observable consequences only
- No god-vessel awareness
- Protocol/method development

**N06-N15 (Turnings 2-3 - CRACK/FORGE):**
- Innocent frame maintained
- Cost escalation visible
- Political/faction pressure increases
- Still no divine awareness

**N16-N25 (Turnings 4-5 - SMELT/CLEAR):**
- Fathom Break occurs (god-vessels BEGIN to suspect)
- Innocent frame cracks but doesn't shatter
- Awareness creeps in gradually
- Orchestration hints appear

**N26-N29 (Turning 6 - BREAK, first 4 books):**
- God-vessels aware but resisting implications
- Counter-Spark presence suspected
- Principles acknowledged but not fully understood

**N30 (Turning 6 - BREAK, final book):**
- GUILTY FRAME ACTIVATES
- All orchestration revealed
- Auditor identity confirmed
- Full metaphysics disclosed
- Dual canon collapses into single truth

- [ ] No character knows they are a god-vessel
- [ ] No character references Divine Principles by name or as entities
- [ ] No character suspects orchestration or observation
- [ ] Soulpulse described as tool/ability, never "divine power"
- [ ] The Seam visible but not explained or given cosmic weight
- [ ] Costs shown through body, never attributed to "divine struggle"
- [ ] All character theories grounded in observable evidence only
- [ ] No "meant to be" or "destined" framing anywhere
- [ ] No narrative voice suggesting cosmic significance
- [ ] Closing maintains innocent frame integrity

## Why This Matters

The dual canon system creates:
1. **Dramatic Irony:** Readers (who know more) watch characters struggle in ignorance
2. **Rereadability:** N01-N29 gain new meaning after N30 revelation
3. **Trust Preservation:** No retcons—innocent frame is TRUE from character perspective
4. **Narrative Tension:** Characters make choices without cosmic context
5. **Payoff Intensity:** N30 recontextualizes 29 novellas of innocent-frame struggle

**Maintaining innocent frame integrity in N01 is NON-NEGOTIABLE.**
